Chenyang Si

Associate Professor
School of Intelligence Science and Technology
Nanjing University

Prof. Chenyang Si is a Tenure-Track Associate Professor at the School of Intelligence Science and Technology, Nanjing University. His research interests span visual understanding and generation, including fundamental architectures for computer vision, video understanding, generative models, video and image generation, as well as acceleration and optimization of generative models. Prior to this, he was a Research Fellow at Nanyang Technological University (NTU), Singapore, and worked as a Research Scientist at the Sea AI Lab of Sea Group.
